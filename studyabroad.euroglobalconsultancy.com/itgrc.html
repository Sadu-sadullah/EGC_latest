About IT Governance Training Courses

Upon completion of these course(s), professionals will be trained in order to achieve certification(s) that demonstrates their expertise in the area of IT Governance, Risk and Compliance.

Overview

  • IT Governance is the process of managing and controlling the use of technology within an organization. It is used to ensure that technology is used in a way that is consistent with the organization's goals and objectives. Major vendors such as IBM, Microsoft provide software and services that help organizations manage their IT infrastructure, develop policies and procedures, and ensure compliance with regulations. Additionally, they provide consulting services to help organizations develop and implement IT Governance strategies.

Course Prerequisites

In order to enroll in the IT governance training, there are no mandatory prerequisites. However, it is advised that students possess some basic knowledge of information technology, business principles, IT governance frameworks, and enterprise risk management. It would also be beneficial for participants to have an understanding of enterprise governance of IT (EGIT).
